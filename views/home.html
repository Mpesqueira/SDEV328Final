About Us

Rice Rice Baby is committed to be the largest local producer of hybrid rice and hybrid rice seeds in the Philippines and tropical Asia. Rice Rice Baby is the first local company and market leader in hybrid rice and hybrid rice seeds exportation through strategic partnership with our internal and external customers. We will sustain organizational excellence by delivering premium quality rice and seeds, ensuring employee participation and growth within the organization, utilizing factual data in decision making and instilling continuous improvement as part of daily job activities.

Rice Rice Baby is committed to the principle that quality is everyone's responsibility, and we guarantee to continually improve and comply with the regulatory and statutory requirements and with the requirements of our Quality Management System.
